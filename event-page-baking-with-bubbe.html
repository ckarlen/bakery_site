Baking with Bubbe

See More Posts

I spent last Thursday in the kitchen baking with Bubbe.

"Bubbe" is Yiddish for grandmother, and with the term of endearment comes the knowledge of the traditional desserts for Rosh Hoshanah, the Jewish New Year: hamentashen, challah bread, apple kugel, almond butter cookies. My bubbe may have been a terrible cook, but Ella Wishnoff, master baker and official "Bubbe" of the baking set, spent the day to teach me some of her secrets from the Old Country.
