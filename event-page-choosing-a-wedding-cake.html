The perfect day deserves the perfect cake.

And we can help you design it. I meet with couples to get a sense of their tastes and preferences, and then go to the sketch book, to create a unique cake especially for your wedding day. Next comes the sampling, where you'll meet me in our kitchen for a private tasting to choose the look, flavors and decorations that will compliment your wedding perfectly.
