The Milner Baking Co.

How it all began...

This is my grandmother, Bertha Milner. She loved sweets of all kinds. When we were little, we called her Nanny Candy because you were always sure to find a roll of Lifesavers or slice of cake whenever you went for a visit. She loved to entertain—cocktail parties, bridge games—it didn’t matter as long as it involved food and lively conversation. The only problem was, Bertha was a terrible cook. She always used to say she’d read anything except a recipe. When I was little, I dreamed of having a life like hers, except I wanted the sweets to be homemade. So came the idea for the Milner Baking Company. A way to have your cake and eat it too.
